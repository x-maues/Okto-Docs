# Why Okto?

Okto is revolutionizing the way we interact with blockchain ecosystems. With its innovative features and robust infrastructure, Okto provides users with seamless cross-chain experiences. Below, we answer some of the most frequently asked questions about **Why Okto?**

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

<details>
  <summary><strong>1. What makes Okto different from other blockchain solutions?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Okto is designed with a focus on <strong>interoperability</strong> and <strong>efficiency</strong>. Unlike traditional blockchain networks, Okto connects multiple chains with minimal friction, allowing for a streamlined and unified experience. Through the <strong>Okto Gateway</strong> and <strong>DTN (Dynamic Transaction Network)</strong>, Okto ensures that your cross-chain operations are executed in a seamless, cost-effective, and secure manner.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Cross-chain Interoperability:</strong> Okto seamlessly transfers assets and performs operations across multiple blockchains.</li>
    <li><strong>Cost-efficient Transactions:</strong> With Okto, users only pay a single, all-inclusive fee for multi-chain operations.</li>
    <li><strong>Security:</strong> Okto uses <strong>MPC-secured nodes</strong> for signing transactions, ensuring robust security across its ecosystem.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>2. How does Okto ensure transaction reliability?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Okto uses a multi-layered approach to ensure transaction reliability, including <strong>transaction orchestration</strong> via the DTN, <strong>liquidity aggregation</strong> from the ULL, and <strong>transaction signing</strong> through the DWN. This system manages every aspect of the transaction lifecycle — from ensuring liquidity for cross-chain bridges to preventing issues like double-spending and chain reorganizations.
  </p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>3. What is the Universal Liquidity Layer (ULL)?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    The <strong>Universal Liquidity Layer (ULL)</strong> aggregates liquidity from various cross-chain solutions like <strong>Socket</strong> and <strong>LayerZero</strong> to provide the best bridging routes. ULL ensures that the user’s assets are moved efficiently and cost-effectively, even across chains with low liquidity.
  </p>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Optimal Route Selection:</strong> ULL analyzes multiple paths to choose the quickest, most secure, and cost-effective route for transactions.</li>
    <li><strong>Reduced Latency:</strong> By aggregating liquidity from multiple solutions, ULL minimizes wait times for asset transfers.</li>
  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>4. Can Okto be used for both crypto and traditional assets?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Yes, Okto supports both <strong>crypto assets</strong> (like USDC, ETH, BNB) and <strong>traditional assets</strong> through tokenized solutions. Whether you want to transfer or stake cryptocurrencies or perform operations across decentralized finance (DeFi) applications, Okto handles both seamlessly.
  </p>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>5. How does Okto make staking easier?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Staking with Okto is made simple through its <strong>DTN orchestration</strong>. Users can stake tokens on multiple chains in just one request, and Okto will handle the underlying complexities such as transaction sequencing, gas optimization, and token swaps. Additionally, Okto ensures that users can stake tokens without worrying about manual operations across different blockchain platforms.
  </p>
  <pre>
    Example Workflow:
    1. Transfer USDC from Ethereum to BNB Chain
    2. Stake the transferred tokens on BNB Chain
    3. Single all-inclusive fee
  </pre>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>6. Is Okto suitable for developers?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Absolutely! Okto offers <strong>robust SDKs</strong> and APIs that make it easy for developers to integrate cross-chain features into their applications. Whether you’re building a DApp or a wallet, Okto provides all the necessary tools to automate and simplify cross-chain transactions.
  </p>
  <ul>

  </ul>
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>7. How does Okto make cross-chain transactions cost-effective?</strong></summary>
  <p>
    Okto combines transaction optimization, fee aggregation, and cross-chain liquidity pooling to ensure that users only pay a single, all-inclusive fee for multi-chain operations. By utilizing its Dynamic Transaction Network (DTN) and Universal Liquidity Layer (ULL), Okto automatically determines the most efficient and cost-effective transaction routes.
  </p>
</details>
